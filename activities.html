Adventures of Bali

Bali Swing
     One of my favorite activities that I did was the "Bali Swing." There are a few different locations around the island but I LOVED the one I went to. There were various different swing heights that have an amazing view of the surrounding nature as well as other photo opportunity spots. It's exhilarating, yet completely safe and controlled. There are many staff members who are super friendly that manage everything and even offer to take your pictures and tell you how to pose. You can even rent a flowy dress or skirt to make your instagram photos even more dynamic.
Duration: This is an all day adventure
Total Cost: About $50 USD for admission plus transportation
Tyra's Tip!
      While there are a lot of different swings available, you want to get there early in the morning or towards sunset to avoid long lines.

White Water Rafting
      Another great activities I did in Bali was white water rafting! Not only do you raft through Bali's beautiful scenery, it is fun and competitive! You get to compete against the other teams to get to the finish!
Duration Early Morning - Mid Day
Total Cost About $100 USD plus transportation
Tyra's Tip!
      Wear clothes that you are comfortable in, because you will get wet!

Waterfalls
      A must do activity is finding all the secret waterfalls around the island! Some require more intense hikes while others have easier trails.
Duration Depends on the trail!
Total Cost Most of them are free, but some charge a $5 admissions fee
Tyra's Tip!
      The waterfall is pretty cold, so don't expect to be able and go swimming. The tides are pretty strong as well.
