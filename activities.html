Activities

Bali Swing
     One of my favorite activities that I did in Bali was white water rafting! Not only do you raft through Bali's beautiful scenery, it is fun and competitive! You get to compete against the other teams to get to the finish!
Duration All day
Total Cost About $50 USD plus transportation
Tyra's Tip!
     Wear clothes that you are comfortable in, because you will get wet!
