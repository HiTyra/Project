Description
      If you find yourself in the center of Bali, you HAVE to get an acai or pitaya bowl. They have the freshest fruits. YUM! Another key food is Mie Goreng, which is a tradition Indonesian food you can find pretty much anywhere
Duration 5-10 min walk from city center
Total Cost ~ $5USD
Tyra's Tip!
      The shops in Ubud are the best for all sorts of fruit bowls! Definitely try all the fruits they have to offer.
