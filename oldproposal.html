What Is my Website About?
My Website is a portfolio of my photography or other forms of artwork.

What Is The Main Objective of my Website?
The Objective of my website is to show more of my creative side and passion for the arts. Especially since I spend a lot of time focusing on school and business side of things, I forget to share this part of my identity with the rest of the world.

Who Is My Target Audience?
My target audience is anyone who wants to get to know a little bit more about my life and experiences! While similar styled to how one would make their portfolio when applying to art schools or creative positions, it is a fun way for me to document important moments in my life and also share with others.

What Content Do I plan to include in each page?
I am still debating on whether or not I want to include multiple types of media on my website or just stick with photography. If I include different forms of work I would have pages such as: photography, watercolor paintings, digital art, acrylic paintings, pen art, sketches, ect... each page would include photos and descriptions of my thought process behind the piece. If I decide to only add photography content I would have pages such as: portraits, nature, lifestyle/travel, product shots, furry friends, ect... each page would include photos relating to the category and maybe more of a blog styled description of where and when the photo took place. What do you think?
