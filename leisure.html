There are so many leisure activities to do on the island of Bali on your day off! Spend the day shopping at the mall or at the local town center. If you're feeling up to it, take a moped ride wherever you go Visiting temples is a must, there are many throughout the island but many of the tourist spots get quite busy. Be sure to take a trip to monkey forest, but beware, they might jump on your head! My personal favorite is sitting by the beach and having a refreshing coconut!

Tyra's Tip!
While Bali is a small island, there are so many activities. Make sure to map out your plans so you take advantage of the time you have.
